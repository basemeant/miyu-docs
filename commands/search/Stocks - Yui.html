Stocks

View stock market information.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!stocks quote <symbol>
Shows basic stats on the given stock.
y!stocks chart <symbol>
Shows a chart for the given stock.
y!stocks price <symbol>
Shows the current price of the given stock.
