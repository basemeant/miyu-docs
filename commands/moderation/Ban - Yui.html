Ban

Bans the given member from the server, removing 7 days of their messages.

Usage:

y!ban <member> {reason}

Related Commands

Unban
Used to unban a user from the server.
Kick
Used to kick a member from the server.
Softban
Bans and immediately unbans a member from the server.
Warn
Used to issue a warning to a member.
