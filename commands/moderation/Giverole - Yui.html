Giverole

Quickly assign a role to a member.

Usage:

y!giverole <member> <role>

Related Commands

Takerole
Used to remove a role from a member.
Autorole
Used to automatically assign one or more roles to members when they join the server.
The autorole command can also be used to assign roles to all existing members in the server.
RAS
Used to assign roles based on reactions added to or removed from a message.
