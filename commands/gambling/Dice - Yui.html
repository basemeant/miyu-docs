Dice

Bet on a roll of dice.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!dice
Shows information about the command.
y!dice <sides>
Rolls a die with the given number of sides.
y!dice <sides> <choice> <bet>
Rolls a die with the given number of sides and bets the given amount of silver on the die landing on the given choice.

More Information

Odds: 1 in [number of sides]
Winnings: (bet * sides) - 20%
Minimum Bet: 100 silver
Maximum Bet: 5,000 silver
