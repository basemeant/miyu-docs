Roulette

Bet on a game of Russian roulette against other members.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!roulette
Shows information about the command.
y!roulette <bet>
Starts a game of Russian roulette with the given buy-in.

More Information

Odds: 1 in [number of players]
Winnings: (bet * players) - 20%
Minimum Bet: 100 silver
Maximum Bet: none
The game will time out if no one joins within 30 seconds.
