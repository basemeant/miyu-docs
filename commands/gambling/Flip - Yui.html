Flip

Bet on a coin flip.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!flip
Shows information about the command.
y!flip heads <bet>
Bets the given amount of silver that the flip will land on heads.
y!flip tails <bet>
Bets the given amount of silver that the flip will land on tails.

More Information

Odds: 1 in 2
Winnings: (bet * 2) - 10%
Minimum Bet: 100 silver
Maximum Bet: 10,000 silver
