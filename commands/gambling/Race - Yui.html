Race

Bet on a horse race.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!race
Shows information about the command.
y!race <bet>
Starts a race and bets the given amount.

More Information

Odds: 1 in [number of racers] or 1 in 5 for solo races
Winnings: (bet * racers) - 20%
Minimum Bet: 1,000 silver
Maximum Bet: none
If no one joins the race, a solo race will start. The maximum bet for solo races is 5000 silver.
