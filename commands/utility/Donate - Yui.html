Donate

Donate to Miyu on Patreon for a 30% bonus in silver from the daily command.

Usage:

y!donate
First time donations of $5 or more are eligible for a reward of 500,000 silver per $5. To claim this reward, visit Miyu's support server and ping a helper. Make sure to link your accounts first.
