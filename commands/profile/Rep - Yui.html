Rep

Give a reputation point to another member.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!rep
Shows information about when you can give another reputation point.
y!rep <member>
Gives the given member a reputation point if you have one to give.
You may give one reputation point every 24 hours.
You cannot give yourself reputation points.
