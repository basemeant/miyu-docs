Bank

Manage your Yui bank account.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!bank
Shows information about your bank account.
y!bank deposit <amount>
Deposits the given amount of gold into your bank.
y!bank deposit all
Deposits all your gold into your bank.
y!bank upgrade
Increases the maximum copper reserve of your bank.

Related Commands

Balance
Used to check one's balance.
Exchange
Used to exchange copper for silver or silver for gold.
