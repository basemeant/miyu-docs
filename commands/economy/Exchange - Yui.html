Exchange

Exchange copper for silver or silver for gold.

Usage:

Command
Description
y!exchange
Shows exchange information.
y!exchange silver <amount>
Exchanges copper for the given amount of silver.
y!exchange silver all
Exchanges all copper for silver.
y!exchange gold <amount>
Exchanges silver for the given amount of gold.
y!exchange gold all
Exchanges all silver for gold.
Exchanges cannot be undone.

Exchange Rates

100 copper = 1 silver
1000 silver = 1 gold
