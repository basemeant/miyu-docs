Donating

How to donate to support Yui's development via Patreon.

Create a Patreon Account

  • If you don't already have a Patreon account, make one at https://www.patreon.com/signup.

Becoming a Patron of Yui

  • After creating your Patreon account, go to https://www.patreon.com/join/YuiBot.
  • Select the subscription tier of your choice.
  • Enter your payment details and finish checkout.

Getting your Patreon Rewards

Desktop

  • After subscribing to a tier, hover your cursor over your profile picture in the top right corner.
  • Click My profile settings
  • Click Connected Apps
  • Next to Discord, click Connect
  • Log into your Discord account in the window that opens.
  • Join Yui's support server.

Mobile

  • After subscribing to a tier, tap the three horizontal lines in the top right corner.
  • Tap My profile settings
  • Tap Apps
  • Next to Discord, click Connect
  • Log into your Discord account in the tab that opens.
  • Join Yui's support server.
If you have the Patreon role in the support server, no further action is required. If not, return to your Patreon profile settings and disconnect your Discord account. Then reconnect it and check again. (Most people have to do this at least once.)
If you don't have the Super Cute role or a role from a higher Patreon tier, you will not be given your silver reward, regardless of whether or not you have donated.
If you're having trouble getting your role in the support server, check to make sure your donation payment went through.
